Switching the year of interest

The switcher provides a possibility change the year of interest. Selection of the year of interest leads to reload of the data values, which are visualized in the tool. This tool gives the user an important function of comparing the both quantitative and spatial characteristics of the data between two available years – 2000 and 2010.
