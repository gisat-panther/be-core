Measuring polygon area

This tool enables to measure areas of drawn polygons in square kilometers. To measure area of any polygon in map, activate the measure tool by clicking on it. The tool colors red and small orange circle appears around the mouse cursor. Measuring starts by single click in map. Draw the polygon you want to measure. The polygon is finished by double click. After activation of measure tool, it also appears small window with measured area. This window can be moved anywhere over application window by dragging. Both drawn line and small window disappear after deactivation of measuring tool by clicking on it again.
