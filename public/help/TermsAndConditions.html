Platform Description


The Platform for Urban Management and Analysis (“PUMA”) is an online geospatial software tool created by the World Bank as part of its East Asia and Pacific Urban Flagship study. PUMA allows Users with no prior experience in geographic information systems to access, analyze and share urban spatial data in an interactive and customizable way. For example, maps and graphs on urban expansion may allow researchers and policy makers to understand broad trends in urbanization, while more detailed data on land use in individual cities can help inform land use planning decisions.
