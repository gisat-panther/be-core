Map layers panel

This panel provides access to all layers that can be displayed in the map window. Each layer could be switched on or off by checking / unchecking the check box in front of the name of the layer.


This panel provides two cards with different modes of layers listing:

  • card with all available layers
  • card with layers selected for visualization
