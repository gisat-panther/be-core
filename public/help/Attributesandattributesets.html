Attributes and attribute sets

The application works at the basis of attributes grouped (mostly thematically based) into groups called attribute sets.

Attributes (or indicators, in other words), describe quantitative values of different phenomena summarized for analytical units. They can be represented by e.g. different LC/LU classes, populations, etc. A typical example of an attribute (indicator) in Land Use analysis is e.g. the formation of built-up area.
