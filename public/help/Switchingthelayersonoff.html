Switching on/off displayed layers

Layer can be switched on / of in the map by unchecking their check boxes in the available layers panel or selected layers panel.

After checking / unchecking the box in front of particular layer, the layer displays in the map or disappears from it. In the selected layers panel, layers can be switched off only.
