Normalization by area

Normalization by total area of analytical unit is set as the default when adding new attributes to the chart attribute list. It is indicated by the text "Area" in the "Normalization" column in the chart configuration dialog.

To normalize the attribute value by total area of analytical unit, select "Area" in the "Norm type" menu and then click the "Change normalization" button at the bottom of the card.

After that, normalization by "Area" will be indicated in the "Normalization" column in the chart configuration dialog:
