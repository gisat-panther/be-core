Selection of analytical units

You can select arbitrary analytical units of your interest and compare them to each other. Analytical units at different hierarchical levels can be also compared.


There are three ways to select analytical units you are interested in:

  • Selection in hierarchical tree of analytical units
  • Selection in map
  • Selection in charts


For multiple selection, hold down the CTRL key during the selection – multiple selection is enabled in all hierarchical trees, maps and charts.

There is also a possibility to make more multiple selections utilizing the selection by the multiple selection tool and to compare between these groups of analytical units.

Selected units will be highlighted by corresponding color in all hierarchical trees of units, maps and all charts at the same time.


You can also make use of the highly interactive temporary selection on mouse over.


The advanced filter tool provides advanced possibilities for the selection of units.
