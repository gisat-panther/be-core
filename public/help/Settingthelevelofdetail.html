Switching the analysis level

The switcher provides a possibility to change the level of the analysis detail, represented by different levels of analytical units. It enables to switch to lower or upper level of analytical units, for which statistics are calculated. The number of provided levels of analytical units (and the level of spatial detail of the data) depends on analytical availability of analytical units boundaries for each territory.


Available levels of analytical units for each territory are bellow:

Gisat mapping:

Cebu City – 4 levels

Hai Phong – 4 levels

Ho Chi Minh City – 3 levels

Surabaya – 4 levels

Ulanbatar – 2 levels


For each territory, highest level of analytical units is represented by AOI extent.


China urban expansion – 4 levels
