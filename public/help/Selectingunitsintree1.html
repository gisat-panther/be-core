Selection of analytical units in hierarchical tree

Click on the name of the unit and it will be selected. For multiple selection, hold down the CTRL key during the selection. Each selection will be highlighted by a corresponding selection color, defined via the color selection panel.


You can also select units of your interest in a map or chart panel. Read more about unit selection here.
