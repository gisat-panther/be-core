Setting analysis characteristics  

This panel lets you specify characteristics of analysis.

Two modes of this panel are available:

  • Basic - this mode is intended only for initial application startup. It offers a selection of the scope, place and theme of analysis.


  • Extended - this mode includes selection of all three variables provided in the basic mode of the panel together with selection of three other additional parameters - year, analysis level and visualization.
