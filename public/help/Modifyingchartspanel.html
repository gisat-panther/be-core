Modifying charts panel

Setting the chart panel content

Chart panel is designed for visualization of incorporated data via various types of charts. This kind of graphical representation of statistical values is the most easily understandable way how to present statistical values to the user. The chart panel is expanded by default, it could be hidden to enlarge the map window and expand again using the hide/expand button.


Following types of charts are available:

  • table (GRID)
  • column chart
  • pie chart
  • scatter chart


There are also incorporated two non-chart visualization possibilities:

  • outline
  • snapshots


The user can affect the appearance of the chart panel by adding , reconfiguring  or removing charts.

Each chart in the panel can be expanded or hidden (via hide/expand button). There is also a possibility to export content of each chart: either as graphics  or as tabular data.
