Exporting charts as graphics

Any chart from the chart panel, as well as any extent outline or snapshot can be exported from the application as graphics by clicking the “Export as PNG” button at the top of the chart that should be exported. The chart will be exported in the form in which it is displayed in the application at the moment of export.
