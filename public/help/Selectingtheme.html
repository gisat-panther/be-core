Selection of the theme of interest

This menu lets you select the theme of your interest. Themes depend on the scope (dataset).

For each scope, the most relevant themes that illustrate land cover characteristics and development as well as population characteristics are listed.


You can choose between three available scopes in the menu:

  •  Themes for local scope:


  •   Themes for national scope:


  •   Themes for regional scope:


To enable the selection of the theme, the scope must be selected first.


Listed themes are further divided into sub-themes, which are represented by visualizations.
