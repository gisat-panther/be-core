Understanding chart types

Four basic types of charts can be prepared in the application:

  • Table
  • Column chart
  • Pie chart
  • Scatter chart


Each type of chart is suitable for slightly different types of analysis. Therefore, when selecting the attributes for charts, consider carefully the selection of particular indicators for the particular chart type.
