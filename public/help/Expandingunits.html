Expanding analytical units

You can expand analytical units to more detailed hierarchical levels. A single parent unit can be expanded by a single clicking on the button in the hierarchical tree of analytical units.

More hierarchical levels of units can be expanded.

>

>

>

You can go on expanding analytical units until the icon disappears and is replaced by the icon. This indicates that units on the lowest hierarchical level have no child units and therefore cannot be further expanded. All units on a distinct hierarchical level can be expanded using the level switcher  in the data exploration settings panel. Please note that expanding huge numbers of analytical units at the same time using the level switcher can take longer. Therefore, if you expect higher numbers of units at a lower hierarchical level, it is preferable to expand only a single parent unit by clicking on the icon in the hierarchical tree.
